The Idea

Modern recommender systems learn from our clicks, views and interactions. But what if recommendations could also learn from people we consciously trust?

Caught & Shared explores a simple idea: recommendation systems should not rely only on behavioral signals. Users should also be able to choose whose perspective influences their digital environment.
